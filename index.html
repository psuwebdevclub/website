Background

The Website Development Club was created to provide Penn State students with the opportunities to learn about website development regardless of academic speciality. Website development is a vital skill that can be applied to many fields, and this club covers technical and non-technical aspects of website development that will help members stand out in the world of business, IT, and computer science.

Projects

PSMG

The club is currently working on a website for the Penn State Music Group - a new student run organization that aims to provide a platform for students to share their music and collaborate with other artists. Feel free to contact us if you are interested in joining the project! This is a great opportunity to learn about the design process and gain experience working on a real-world project.

Paid Web Dev Contracts

Want to make some money and gain experience? We are looking for students interested in working on paid web development contracts. If you are interested, please fill out the form below and we will contact you with more information. New contracts typically get grabbed within a few hours, so be sure to check GroupMe often for the latest opportunities like our contract with the PSU iGEM team.
